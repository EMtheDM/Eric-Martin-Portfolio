About Me

My name is Eric Martin. I'm a Full Stack Developer located in Denver, CO. I have a passion for intuitive websites. I have over 10 years of customer service experience that I bring to each and every project. I truly am an end-user focused developer.


I graduated from UNLV with a Film degree originally as I love the process of creation. I took that same passion and bring it to my web development.


I am an extremely well organized and detailed individual. I am quick to think on my feet and problem solve with the best of them. I'm a fan of video games, D&D, pretty much every sport, and of course movies!


Interested in the entire stack of development and working on ambitious projects with positive people.
